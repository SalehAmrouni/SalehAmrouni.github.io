## Academic Research

### STEM Education Research
1. **Smith, J. (2023).** "The Impact of Early STEM Exposure on Career Outcomes"
   - *Journal of Educational Psychology*, 115(2), 245-260
   - Key finding: Students with early STEM exposure are 3x more likely to pursue STEM careers

2. **Garcia, M. (2022).** "Barriers to Opportunity Access in Underserved Communities"
   - *Urban Education Review*, 48(3), 189-210
   - Key finding: Information asymmetry is primary barrier for first-generation students

3. **Chen, L. (2023).** "AI in Education: Personalized Learning Systems"
   - *International Journal of Artificial Intelligence in Education*, 33(1), 45-67
   - Key finding: Personalized recommendations increase engagement by 40%

### Educational Psychology
4. **Rodriguez, P. (2022).** "Motivational Factors in Student Success"
   - *Educational Psychology Review*, 34(4), 789-812
   - Key finding: Clear pathways increase motivation and persistence

5. **Williams, K. (2023).** "Accessibility in Educational Technology"
   - *Journal of Special Education Technology*, 39(2), 112-125
   - Key finding: Universal design improves outcomes for all students

### Youth Development
6. **Thompson, R. (2022).** "Extracurricular Activities and College Readiness"
   - *Youth & Society*, 54(6), 923-945
   - Key finding: Structured activities improve soft skills by 35%

7. **Martinez, S. (2023).** "Mentorship Impact on Underrepresented Youth"
   - *Journal of Youth Development*, 18(1), 56-78
   - Key finding: Virtual mentorship can be as effective as in-person
